Catalog


The goal of our project ‘Beat Cancer with NLP’ is to build a Clinical Data Analytics Platform, which leverages NLP to transform unstructured free text clinical note data into structured form that is critical to cancer research. The data curation application will enable Doctors, Researchers, and their staff to more efficiently capture core data elements relating to patients, depositing that knowledge into a centralized database that can be used across the entire Institute. This will enable Clinicians, Clinical Researchers, and Data Scientists to shift their focus from data capture and cleaning to the analysis of important cancer related concepts like treatment history, patient outcomes, diagnosis, and staging over a patients’ clinical timeline. We built the system in such a way that our NLP algorithms will provide recommendations to users, capture feedback from the user on the quality of the NLP assist feature, and allow for us to iteratively improve our algorithms over time.
