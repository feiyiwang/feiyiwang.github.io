Alphabetical list of part-of-speech tags


No.    
Tag        
中文
Description & Examples  
1. CC 连词 Coordinating conjunction -> and or but if while although
2. CD 数词 Cardinal number -> twenty-four fourth 1991 12:24
3. DT 限定词 Determiner -> the a some most every no
4. EX 存在量词 Existential -> there there's
5. FW 外来词 Foreign word -> dolce ersatz esprit guo maitre
6. IN 介词连词 Preposition or subordinating conjunction -> on of at with by into under
7. JJ 形容词 Adjective -> new good high special big local
8. JJR 比较级形容词 Adjective, comparative -> bleaker braver breezier brisker
9. JJS 最高级形容词 Adjective, superlative -> calmest cheapest choicest classiest
10. LS 标记 List item marker -> A A. C C. First J K
11. MD 情态动词 Modal -> can cannot could couldn't
12. NN 名词 Noun, singular or mass -> year home time education
13. NNS 名词复数 Noun, plural -> undergraduates scotches
14. NNP 专有名词 Proper noun, singular -> Alison Africa April Washington
15. NNPS 专有名词复数 Proper noun, plural -> Americans Americas
16. PDT 前限定词 Predeterminer -> all both half many
17. POS 所有格标记 Possessive ending -> ' 's
18. PRP 人称代词 Personal pronoun -> I me you she her herself us itself
19. PRP$ 所有格 Possessive pronoun -> hers his mine yours
20. RB 副词 Adverb -> occasionally unwittingly maddeningly
21. RBR 副词比较级 Adverb, comparative -> further gloomier grander
22. RBS 副词最高级 Adverb, superlative -> best bluntest earliest
23. RP 虚词 Particle -> aboard about across along apart
24. SYM 符号 Symbol -> % # & ! " ( }
25. TO to
26. UH 感叹词 Interjection -> Goodbye Gosh Goody Yike Wow
27. VB 动词 Verb, base form -> like, have, go, buy, write
28. VBD 动词过去式 Verb, past tense -> liked, had, went, bought, wrote
29. VBG 动词现在分词 Verb, gerund or present participle -> liking, having, going, buying, writing
30. VBN 动词过去分词 Verb, past participle -> liked, had, gone, bought, written
31. VBP 动词现在式非第三人称时态     Verb, non-3rd person singular present -> like, have, go, buy, write
32. VBZ 动词现在式第三人称时态 Verb, 3rd person singular present -> likes, has, goes, buys, writes
33. WDT Wh限定词 Wh-determiner -> who which when what where how
34. WP Wh代词 Wh-pronoun -> that what whatever
35. WP$ Wh代词所有格 Possessive wh-pronoun -> whose
36. WRB Wh副词 Wh-adverb -> when where why whence whereby wherein
